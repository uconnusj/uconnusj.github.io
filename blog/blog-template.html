Professor Metin Coşgel Blog Post

Metin Coşgel is a professor in the Economics Department at the University of Connecticut with a focus on research in historical economics. Originally from Turkey, Professor Coşgel attended Boğaziçi University in Istanbul, where he obtained an undergraduate degree in economics. Educational opportunities then led him to the United States at age 22, where he continued his graduate studies at the University of Iowa, obtaining a PhD in economics.
